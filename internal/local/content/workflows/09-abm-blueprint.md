# Workflow: Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Blueprint

## Purpose
A structured program for targeting high-value accounts with personalized, multi-channel campaigns. ABM flips the funnel — instead of casting a wide net and filtering down, you start with the accounts you want and build campaigns specifically for them.

## When to Use This Workflow
- Average deal size >$25K/year
- Clear, identifiable target accounts
- Sales and marketing alignment exists (or is being built)
- Enterprise or mid-market selling motion
- Orchestrator routes here for "ABM" or "enterprise targeting" requests

---

## Phase 1: ABM Strategy Foundation

### Step 1.1: Choose Your ABM Tier

| Tier | Target Accounts | Personalization | Investment | Best For |
|------|:--------------:|:--------------:|:----------:|---------|
| **1:1 ABM (Strategic)** | 5-25 | Fully customized per account | Very High | $100K+ deals, named accounts |
| **1:Few ABM (Cluster)** | 25-100 | Customized per segment/cluster | High | $25K-100K deals, industry segments |
| **1:Many ABM (Programmatic)** | 100-500 | Personalized at scale via automation | Medium | $10K-25K deals, ICP-matched accounts |

### Step 1.2: Account Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring Guide |
|-----------|:------:|-------------|
| **Revenue fit** (can they afford your solution?) | High | Annual revenue, funding stage, budget signals |
| **Need fit** (do they have the problem you solve?) | High | Industry, tech stack, growth signals |
| **Relationship** (do we have an in?) | Medium | Existing contacts, mutual connections, past engagement |
| **Strategic value** (would this logo open doors?) | Medium | Brand recognition, reference potential |
| **Timing** (are they in-market now?) | Medium | Job postings, technology changes, funding rounds |

### Step 1.3: Account Tier Assignment

| Tier | Accounts | Criteria | Monthly Investment |
|------|:--------:|---------|:-:|
| **Tier 1** (Strategic) | 10-25 | Highest revenue potential + relationship | $500-2,000/account |
| **Tier 2** (Cluster) | 25-50 | Strong fit, grouped by industry/need | $100-500/cluster |
| **Tier 3** (Programmatic) | 50-200 | ICP match, automated personalization | $20-50/account |

## Phase 3: ABM Campaign Execution

### Step 3.1: The Multi-Channel Play (Per Account/Cluster)

| Channel | Tactic | Personalization Level | Frequency |
|---------|--------|:---:|:---------:|
| **LinkedIn (organic)** | Engage with their content, share relevant insights | High | Daily |
| **LinkedIn (ads)** | Targeted ads to buying committee | Medium | Ongoing |
| **Email (1:1)** | Personalized outreach from AE or exec | Very High | 2-3 per sequence |
| **Direct mail** | Physical package or handwritten note | Very High | 1-2 per quarter |
| **Content** | Account-specific or industry-specific content | High | Monthly |
| **Events** | Exclusive invitations, private dinners, workshops | Very High | Quarterly |
| **Digital ads** | Display retargeting to account's IP/cookie pool | Low-Medium | Ongoing |
| **Gifting** | Thoughtful, personalized gifts (not swag) | Very High | Strategic moments |

### Step 3.2: ABM Email Sequence (Tier 1 — 1:1)

| Email | Day | Subject | Content |
|:-----:|:---:|---------|---------|
| 1 | 0 | "[Company]-specific insight about [challenge]" | Lead with research. Show you understand their world. NO pitch. |
| 2 | 5 | "How [similar company] solved [same challenge]" | Relevant case study. Social proof from their industry/size. |
| 3 | 12 | "[Mutual connection] suggested we connect" | Leverage relationships. Personal, warm, specific. |
| 4 | 20 | "Quick thought on [their recent news/initiative]" | React to a trigger event. Show you're paying attention. |
| 5 | 30 | "Worth 15 minutes? Specific agenda inside." | Clear ask, specific value for the call, respect for their time. |

### Step 3.3: ABM Content Matrix

| Buying Stage | Content Type | Personalization |
|-------------|-------------|:-:|
| **Unaware** | Industry trend report, thought leadership | Industry-level |
| **Problem-aware** | Problem-specific blog post, benchmark data | Segment-level |
| **Solution-aware** | Solution comparison, ROI calculator | Persona-level |
| **Evaluating** | Case study (same industry/size), product demo | Account-level |
| **Decision** | Custom proposal, executive business case | Fully custom |

## Phase 4: ABM Measurement

### ABM Metrics (Different from Demand Gen)

| Metric | What It Measures | Target |
|--------|-----------------|:------:|
| **Account engagement score** | Buying committee activity across channels | Increasing trend |
| **Accounts reached** (% of target list engaged) | Program coverage | >80% |
| **Buying committee coverage** | % of committee members with at least 1 touchpoint | >60% |
| **Pipeline generated** | $ in pipeline from target accounts | $ |
| **Pipeline velocity** | Time from first touch to opportunity creation | Decreasing |
| **Win rate** (ABM accounts vs. non-ABM) | ABM effectiveness | ABM > non-ABM |
| **Average deal size** (ABM vs. non-ABM) | ABM quality | ABM > non-ABM |
| **Revenue from target accounts** | Bottom-line impact | $ |

### Account Engagement Scoring

| Activity | Points | Decay |
|----------|:------:|:-----:|
