# Workflow: Product Launch Playbook

## Purpose
Execute a structured product or feature launch that maximizes impact, builds anticipation, and converts interest into revenue. This workflow covers the complete launch cycle from pre-launch seeding through post-launch optimization.

## When to Use This Workflow
- Launching a new product or service
- Launching a significant new feature or tier
- Relaunching or repositioning an existing product
- Running a time-limited offer or promotion
- Orchestrator routes here for any "launch" request

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch Strategy (4-8 Weeks Before Launch)

### Step 1.1: Launch Brief

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What are we launching?** | |
| **Who is it for?** (ICP) | |
| **What problem does it solve?** | |
| **Awareness level of target audience:** | Schwartz level 1-5 |
| **Big Idea for the launch:** | |
| **Launch date:** | |
| **Launch type:** | Evergreen / Time-limited / Seasonal |
| **Revenue target:** | $ in first [30/60/90] days |
| **Launch hypothesis:** | "We believe [action] will [outcome] because [reason]" |
| **Kill criteria:** | "If [metric] < [threshold] after [time], we pivot" |

### Step 1.2: Offer Design (Pre-Launch)

Before any marketing, nail the offer using the Offer Design Lab:

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Core offer:** | What they get |
| **Bonuses (value stack):** | Additional items that increase perceived value |
| **Guarantee:** | Risk reversal strategy |
| **Urgency mechanism:** | Early-bird pricing / Limited spots / Deadline |
| **Pricing:** | Launch price vs. eventual price |
| **Hormozi Value Equation score:** | Must be >5.0 to proceed |

### Step 1.3: Launch Content Calendar

| Timeframe | Activity | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Week -8 to -6 | Topic seeding | Plant ideas about the problem in audience's mind |
| Week -6 to -4 | Authority building | Establish expertise on the topic |
| Week -4 to -2 | Anticipation building | Tease the solution without revealing |
| Week -2 to -1 | Waitlist / early access | Capture high-intent leads |
| Launch week | Full launch sequence | Convert interest to sales |
| Week +1 to +2 | Social proof + urgency | Close fence-sitters |
| Week +2 to +4 | Post-launch optimization | Iterate on conversion |

## Phase 2: Pre-Launch Execution (2-4 Weeks Before)

### Step 2.1: Seed Content (Weeks -8 to -4)

Create content that highlights the PROBLEM your launch solves:

| Content Piece | Format | Channel | Purpose |
|--------------|--------|---------|---------|
| "The hidden cost of [problem]" | Post/article | LinkedIn, blog | Problem awareness |
| Customer pain story | Social post | LinkedIn, X | Empathy + relatability |
| Industry data/trend | Thread | X, LinkedIn | Authority |
| "What I learned building [solution]" | Personal story | LinkedIn, newsletter | Behind-the-scenes |
| "Why most [solutions] fail" | Educational | Blog, LinkedIn | Positioning |

### Step 2.2: Build Anticipation (Weeks -4 to -2)

| Tactic | Execution | Goal |
|--------|----------|------|
| **Teaser content** | "Working on something I'm excited about..." | Curiosity |
| **Behind-the-scenes** | Screenshots, prototypes, development stories | Transparency |
| **Waitlist page** | Simple LP: headline + email capture + "be first to know" | Lead capture |
| **Early feedback** | Share with 5-10 trusted contacts, gather reactions | Validation |
| **Social proof seeding** | Beta tester results, early access stories | Credibility |

### Step 2.3: Email Pre-Launch Sequence

| Email # | Timing | Subject | Content |
|:-------:|--------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Day -14 | "[Teaser] Something big is coming" | Open a story loop. Hint at the problem you're solving. |
| 2 | Day -10 | "The story behind [product]" | Share your origin story — why you built this. |
| 3 | Day -7 | "Here's what [early users] are saying" | Beta results, early testimonials, sneak peek. |
| 4 | Day -3 | "You're on the list — here's what's coming" | Reveal the offer structure (not pricing yet). |
| 5 | Day -1 | "Tomorrow at [time]" | Final anticipation + early-bird benefits. |

## Phase 3: Launch Week Execution

### Step 3.1: Launch Day

| Time | Action | Channel |
|------|--------|---------|
| Morning | Send launch email (the full reveal) | Email |
| Morning | Publish launch post with full story | LinkedIn |
| Morning | Launch thread | X/Twitter |
| Midday | Follow-up engagement on all posts | Social |
| Afternoon | Personal DMs to Dream 20 most-likely buyers | LinkedIn, email |
| Evening | "Day 1 update" story post | Social |

### Step 3.2: Launch Email Sequence

| Email # | Day | Subject Line Template | Focus |
|:-------:|:---:|----------------------|-------|
| 1 | Day 0 | "[Product] is live — here's everything you need to know" | Full offer reveal, value stack, early-bird pricing |
| 2 | Day 1 | "The story of [first customer result]" | Social proof, case study |
| 3 | Day 2 | "[X] people have already joined — here's what they're saying" | Social proof momentum |
| 4 | Day 3 | "Your top 5 questions about [product], answered" | FAQ, objection handling |
| 5 | Day 4 | "What [product] actually looks like inside" | Demo, walkthrough, behind-the-scenes |
| 6 | Day 5 | "This is your last chance at [early-bird price]" | Urgency, deadline reminder |
| 7 | Day 6 | "Final call — [X hours] left" | Scarcity, urgency, strongest proof |
| 8 | Day 6 (PM) | "Doors closing at midnight" | Last-chance email |

### Step 3.3: Social Content During Launch Week

| Day | Content | Format |
|-----|---------|--------|
| Day 0 | Launch announcement + full story | Long-form post |
| Day 1 | Customer result / early feedback | Social proof post |
| Day 2 | "Behind the scenes of building this" | Personal story |
| Day 3 | Educational content related to the problem | Framework post |
| Day 4 | FAQ / myth-busting | Q&A format |
| Day 5 | "What customers are saying" | Testimonial roundup |
| Day 6 | Urgency + final CTA | Direct offer post |

## Phase 4: Post-Launch (Weeks +1 to +4)

### Step 4.1: Immediate Post-Launch (Week +1)

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Send "thank you" email to all buyers | Retention, reduce buyer's remorse |
| Publish launch results transparency post | Build audience trust, social proof |
| Collect feedback from first buyers | Product improvement, testimonials |
| DM non-converters: "What held you back?" | Objection research for next launch |
| Update landing page with social proof | Ongoing conversion optimization |

### Step 4.2: Post-Launch Optimization (Weeks +2 to +4)

| Experiment | What to Test | Success Metric |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| Landing page headline | 3 headline variants | Conversion rate |
| Price point | If early-bird expired, test final pricing | Revenue per visitor |
| Guarantee strength | Stronger vs. softer guarantee | Conversion + refund rate |
| Ad creative (if running ads) | Top 5 hooks from launch content | CPC, CPA, ROAS |
| Email nurture for non-buyers | New angle, different objection handling | Delayed conversion rate |

### Step 4.3: Launch Postmortem

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **Revenue vs. target:** | $ achieved / $ target = % |
| **Customers acquired:** | # |
| **Best-performing channel:** | |
| **Best-performing email:** | (subject line, open rate, click rate) |
| **Best-performing content piece:** | (title, engagement, conversions) |
| **Top objection from non-buyers:** | |
| **Biggest surprise:** | |
| **What to do differently next time:** | |
| **Hypothesis validated?** | Yes / No / Partially |

## Phase 5: Launch Readiness Checklist

### Pre-Launch (T-7 days)

- [ ] Offer designed and Value Equation scored (>5.0)
- [ ] Landing/sales page live and tested
- [ ] Payment processing tested (end-to-end)
- [ ] Email sequences written and scheduled
- [ ] Social content calendar finalized
- [ ] Waitlist/pre-launch leads captured
- [ ] Early testimonials or beta results ready
- [ ] FAQ document prepared
- [ ] Support/onboarding process ready for new customers
- [ ] Analytics/tracking in place (UTMs, conversion tracking)

### Launch Day (T-0)

- [ ] Launch email sent
- [ ] Social posts published
- [ ] Landing page live with correct pricing/links
- [ ] Payment flow verified (make a test purchase)
- [ ] Support inbox monitored
- [ ] Real-time engagement on social (first 60 minutes critical)

### Post-Launch (T+7)

- [ ] Thank-you emails sent to buyers
- [ ] Feedback collected from first customers
- [ ] Non-buyer follow-up sent
- [ ] Social proof updated on landing page
- [ ] Launch postmortem completed
- [ ] Learnings documented for next launch
