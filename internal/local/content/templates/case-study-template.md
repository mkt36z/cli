# Case Study / Customer Story Template

Transform customer results into your most powerful marketing asset. Case studies are Level 4 proof (the second strongest in the Proof Hierarchy) and serve as the backbone of your social proof stack across every channel.

---

## Part 1: The Interview Guide

### Pre-Interview Preparation

Before the customer call, gather:
- [ ] Their before-state data (metrics when they started)
- [ ] Their after-state data (current metrics)
- [ ] Timeline of their journey with you
- [ ] Any quotes from emails/messages they've sent you

### Interview Questions (30-45 minutes)

#### The Before (5-10 min)
1. "What was your situation before you started working with us / using [product]?"
2. "What was the biggest challenge you were facing?"
3. "How was this problem affecting your business/life? Be specific — numbers, time, stress."
4. "What had you tried before that didn't work?"
5. "What was your initial skepticism or hesitation?"

#### The Decision (5 min)
6. "What made you decide to try [product/service]?"
7. "What almost stopped you from buying?"
8. "What was the tipping point?"

#### The Experience (10-15 min)
9. "Walk me through what happened in the first [week/month]."
10. "What was the first result you noticed?"
11. "What surprised you most about the experience?"
12. "Was there a specific moment where you thought 'this is working'?"

#### The Results (10 min)
13. "What specific results have you achieved?" (Push for numbers)
14. "How has this changed your business/day-to-day?"
15. "What would you say to someone who's on the fence about [product/service]?"
16. "If you had to summarize the experience in one sentence, what would you say?"

## Part 4: Case Study Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] **Specificity:** All results include specific numbers, not vague claims
- [ ] **Timeline:** Clear timeframe from start to results
- [ ] **Customer voice:** 3+ direct quotes from the customer (not paraphrased)
- [ ] **Before state:** Reader can clearly picture the "before" situation
- [ ] **Relatability:** Target reader sees themselves in this customer's story
- [ ] **Credibility:** Customer name, title, and company included (with permission)
- [ ] **Proof level:** Meets Level 4+ on the Proof Hierarchy
- [ ] **Disclaimers:** "Results may vary" or equivalent where required
- [ ] **Permission:** Written approval from customer to publish
- [ ] **Photography:** Customer headshot or company logo available
- [ ] **CTA:** Clear next step for readers inspired by this story
