# Pitch / Proposal Framework

## Purpose
Standardized templates for agency pitches and proposals that win business. Covers the full journey from pitch deck to formal proposal to capabilities presentation.

---

## Part 1: The Pitch Deck (10-12 Slides)

### Slide Structure

| Slide # | Title | Content | Time |
|:-------:|-------|---------|:----:|
| 1 | **Title Slide** | Agency name, client name, date, "Marketing Partnership Proposal" | 15s |
| 2 | **The Challenge** | Mirror their problem back to them (from discovery call). Use their words. | 2 min |
| 3 | **The Opportunity** | What's possible if this is solved. Quantify the upside. | 1.5 min |
| 4 | **Our Approach** | Your methodology / framework (3-5 steps). NOT a list of services. | 2 min |
| 5 | **The Strategy** | Specific to THEIR situation. Show you've done the homework. | 3 min |
| 6 | **The 90-Day Plan** | Month-by-month actions with milestones | 2 min |
| 7 | **Case Study** | Most relevant client story: challenge → approach → results | 2 min |
| 8 | **The Team** | Who they'll work with. Names, faces, relevant experience. | 1 min |
| 9 | **Investment** | Pricing options (good/better/best). Frame as investment, not cost. | 2 min |
| 10 | **What Happens Next** | Clear next steps + timeline to start | 1 min |
| 11 | **Why Us** | 3 differentiators. Not features — reasons to trust. | 1 min |
| 12 | **Q&A** | Open discussion | 5 min |

### Pitch Deck Rules

1. **Less text, more conversation** — the deck supports your pitch, it IS NOT the pitch
2. **Lead with THEIR problem, not your capabilities** — they don't care what you do until they believe you understand what they need
3. **Show, don't tell** — case studies, data, examples > claims
4. **One idea per slide** — if you need two columns, you need two slides
5. **Customize at least 3 slides** — slides 2, 3, and 5 must be client-specific

## Part 2: The Formal Proposal Document

### Proposal Template

#### 1. Executive Summary (1 page)

```
Dear [Client Name],

Thank you for the opportunity to present our proposal for [engagement description].

Based on our conversations, we understand your primary challenges are:
1. [Challenge 1]
2. [Challenge 2]
3. [Challenge 3]

We propose a [duration] engagement focused on [primary objective], with a target
outcome of [quantified goal].

Our investment recommendation: [$X/month] for [duration].

We look forward to partnering with you.
```

#### 2. Situation Analysis (1-2 pages)

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Current state** | Summary of their marketing today (from discovery) |
| **Challenges identified** | Top 3-5 challenges with supporting evidence |
| **Opportunities** | What's possible with the right strategy |
| **Competitive landscape** | Brief competitive positioning observation |

#### 3. Proposed Strategy (2-3 pages)

| Element | Description |
|---------|-----------|
| **Strategic thesis** | One-sentence strategy statement |
| **Target audience** | ICP definition (confirm their understanding) |
| **Channel strategy** | Which channels, why, how they work together |
| **Content strategy** | Content themes, cadence, types |
| **Campaign plan** | Key campaigns for first 90 days |
| **Success metrics** | How we'll measure success (KPIs + targets) |

#### 4. Scope of Work (1-2 pages)

| Deliverable | Frequency | Description | Included In |
|------------|:---------:|-------------|:-----------:|
| | Monthly | | Tier 1 / 2 / 3 |
| | Weekly | | Tier 1 / 2 / 3 |
| | Quarterly | | Tier 2 / 3 |
| | As needed | | Tier 3 |

#### 5. Timeline

| Phase | Weeks | Key Milestones |
|-------|:-----:|---------------|
| **Onboarding + Discovery** | 1-2 | Kickoff, audit, strategy development |
| **Foundation** | 3-4 | Content calendar, initial campaigns, brand alignment |
| **Execution** | 5-8 | Full campaign launch, content engine running |
| **Optimization** | 9-12 | Data-driven refinement, scaling |
| **Review** | 12 | QBR, renewal discussion |

#### 6. Investment Options

| Package | Scope | Monthly Investment | Contract |
|---------|-------|--------------------|----------|
| **Essential** | Core deliverables | $/month | 6-month |
| **Growth** | Essential + expanded scope | $/month | 6-month |
| **Premium** | Full-service partnership | $/month | 12-month |

**What's included in all packages:**
- Dedicated account manager
- Monthly reporting
- [X] strategy calls per month
- Access to [tools/platforms]

**Payment terms:** [Net 15 / Net 30], [Monthly / Quarterly invoicing]

#### 7. Team

| Name | Role | Relevant Experience |
|------|------|-------------------|
| | Account Director | |
| | Strategist | |
| | Content Lead | |
| | Channel Specialist | |

#### 8. Case Studies (2-3)

For each case study:

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Client** | Industry, size, situation |
| **Challenge** | What they were facing |
| **What we did** | Strategy + execution summary |
| **Results** | Quantified outcomes |

#### 9. Terms and Conditions

| Term | Details |
|------|---------|

## Part 4: Proposal Winning Tactics

### Before the Pitch

| Action | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Audit their website and social media | Show you've done homework |
| Find a quick win to present | Demonstrate immediate value |
| Research the decision maker on LinkedIn | Personalize your approach |
| Prepare 3 pointed questions to ask | Shows strategic thinking |
| Rehearse the pitch with your team | Smooth delivery builds confidence |

### During the Pitch

| Tactic | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Mirror their language** | Use words from your discovery call |
| **Lead with insight, not credentials** | "We noticed X about your market" > "We've been in business for Y years" |
| **Address the elephant in the room** | If they have concerns, raise them before they do |
| **Leave time for questions** | 30% of time should be discussion, not presentation |
| **End with a clear next step** | "If this resonates, the next step is [specific action]" |

### After the Pitch

| Action | Timeline |
|--------|:--------:|
| Send thank-you email with deck attached | Same day |
| Follow up with additional insight / quick win | Day +2 |
| Send formal proposal (if not included in pitch) | Day +3 |
| Follow up on decision timeline | Day +7 |
| If no response, add value (share relevant content) | Day +14 |
