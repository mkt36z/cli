## 8. Quality Checklist (Pre-Submission)

Before submitting, verify:

- [ ] **Awareness level match:** Copy speaks to the specified awareness level
- [ ] **Delete Test passed:** No paragraph can be removed without weakening the piece
- [ ] **Bar Stool Test passed:** Reads like a smart friend talking, not a brochure
- [ ] **Specificity Test:** >80% of claims are specific (numbers, names, timeframes)
- [ ] **Proof stack:** Every major claim supported at Level 3+
- [ ] **Single CTA:** One clear action the reader should take
- [ ] **Customer language used:** Copy uses Language Bank phrases, not jargon
- [ ] **Headline formula applied:** Hook follows one of the 25 proven formulas
- [ ] **Channel format compliant:** Meets platform-specific requirements
