# Client Reporting Templates

## Purpose
Standardized templates for agency client reporting — monthly performance reports that demonstrate value, build trust, and drive strategic conversations. Designed to be clear enough for C-suite and detailed enough for marketing managers.

---

## Part 1: Monthly Client Report Structure

### Report Outline

| Section | Pages | Audience Focus |
|---------|:-----:|---------------|
| 1. Executive Summary | 1 | C-suite — "Did marketing work this month?" |
| 2. KPI Dashboard | 1 | All — visual performance at a glance |
| 3. Channel Performance | 2-3 | Marketing manager — detailed channel metrics |
| 4. Campaign Highlights | 1-2 | All — what we did and what happened |
| 5. Content Performance | 1 | Marketing manager — which content performed |
| 6. Budget Utilization | 1 | Finance / C-suite — how money was spent |
| 7. Insights & Recommendations | 1 | All — what we learned and what's next |
| 8. Next Month Plan | 1 | All — upcoming priorities and deliverables |

## Part 3: Reporting Best Practices

### Report Design Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Lead with business outcomes** | Revenue impact → channel metrics → activity metrics |
| **Visual hierarchy** | Most important numbers are biggest and first |
| **Red/Yellow/Green** | Every metric has a status indicator |
| **Trend over snapshot** | Always show MoM and vs. target |
| **"So what?"** | Every data point has an implied action |
| **One page per section** | If it doesn't fit on one page, simplify |

### Reporting Don'ts

| Don't | Why | Do Instead |
|-------|-----|-----------|
| Don't list every social post | Activity ≠ results | Summarize by theme + top performers |
| Don't hide bad numbers | Clients lose trust when they discover them | Present honestly with your plan to improve |
| Don't use marketing jargon | Client is not a marketer | Use business language: revenue, pipeline, customers |
| Don't present data without insight | Data without story is just noise | Every chart needs a "so what" sentence |
| Don't wait to share bad news | Monthly report is too late for crisis | Flag issues in real-time, use report for context |

## Part 4: Report Delivery Process

| Step | Timing | Owner |
|------|:------:|-------|
| Pull data from all platforms | Month-end + 2 days | Analyst |
| Draft report | Month-end + 4 days | Account Manager |
| Internal review | Month-end + 5 days | Account Director |
| Send report to client | Month-end + 7 days | Account Manager |
| Schedule report walkthrough call | Within 3 days of sending | Account Manager |
| Log any action items from discussion | Same day as call | Account Manager |
