# Marketing Budget Template

A practical budget framework for solo founders and lean teams. This template scales from $0/month (pure organic) to $15K+/month (full-channel investment).

---

## 1. Budget Sizing by Founder Stage

### How Much Should You Spend?

| Founder Stage | Monthly Revenue | Recommended Marketing Budget | % of Revenue |
|--------------|:--------------:|:---------------------------:|:-----------:|
| **Zero** (Pre-revenue) | $0 | $0-200 (time > money) | N/A |
| **Early** (<$10K/mo) | $1-10K | $200-1,000 | 10-15% |
| **Growing** ($10-50K/mo) | $10-50K | $1,000-5,000 | 10-12% |
| **Scaling** ($50-500K/mo) | $50-500K | $5,000-50,000 | 8-10% |
| **Optimizing** ($500K+/mo) | $500K+ | $50,000+ | 5-10% |

**Rule:** Never spend money on marketing before you've validated PMF with organic/manual efforts.

## 5. Quarterly Budget Review

### Q___ Review

| Question | Answer | Action |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Which channel has the lowest CAC? | | Increase budget |
| Which channel has the highest CAC? | | Reduce budget or optimize |
| Are we testing enough new channels? | | Allocate 5-10% to experiments |
| What's our blended ROAS? | | Target: 3x+ |
| Are any channels showing diminishing returns? | | Cap spend, diversify |
| What's the biggest budget waste? | | Cut or reallocate |

### Budget Reallocation Decision Framework

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Channel CAC < target and scaling | Increase budget 20-30% next month |
| Channel CAC > 2x target | Cut budget 50% or pause, diagnose |
| New channel test showing promise | Graduate from test budget to growth budget |
| Channel hitting diminishing returns | Cap at current spend, reallocate excess |
| Seasonal opportunity | Create temporary budget from testing fund |

## 7. The $0 Budget Playbook

When you have no marketing budget, invest time instead:

| Activity | Time Investment | Expected Impact | Timeline to Results |
|----------|:--------------:|----------------|:------------------:|
| LinkedIn posting (3-5x/week) | 3-5 hrs/week | Audience building, leads | 60-90 days |
| Blog/SEO content (2-4x/month) | 8-12 hrs/month | Organic traffic | 90-180 days |
| Community engagement | 2-3 hrs/week | Network, referrals | 30-60 days |
| Email newsletter (weekly) | 2-3 hrs/week | Nurture, conversion | 30-60 days |
| Warm outreach (First 10 Customers) | 5-10 hrs/week | Direct sales | 7-30 days |
| Content repurposing | 2-3 hrs/week | 10x content output | Immediate |

**Total time investment:** 20-30 hrs/week (this IS your full-time job at pre-revenue stage)

---

## Usage Notes

- **Update monthly:** Track actual vs. planned spend
- **Review ROI quarterly:** Reallocate based on channel performance
- **Always maintain a test budget:** 5-10% for experimenting with new channels/tactics
- **Track CAC by channel:** Not just blended — channel-level CAC reveals where to invest
- **When in doubt, invest in content:** It compounds. Ads stop the moment you stop paying.
