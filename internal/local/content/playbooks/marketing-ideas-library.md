# 139 Marketing Ideas Library — Categorized by Stage & Channel

*Source: MarketingSkills `marketing-ideas` skill — 17-category ideation library with stage mapping (Roadmap item C14)*

> When you're stuck on what to do next, scan the category that matches your stage and channel. Each idea includes effort level and expected impact to help you prioritize.

---

## How to Use This Library

1. **Find your stage** — Pre-launch, Early (0-100 customers), Growth (100-1K), Scale (1K+)
2. **Pick your channel constraint** — What channel are you investing in right now?
3. **Select 2-3 ideas** from the matching category
4. **Score each** using the 8-Factor Opportunity Score from Channel Operator before executing
5. **Track results** in your marketing calendar

**Effort levels:** L = Low (< 1 day) | M = Medium (1-3 days) | H = High (1+ week)
**Impact levels:** 1 = Incremental | 2 = Meaningful | 3 = High | 4 = Transformative
