## Part 4: Fintech / Financial Services

### Fintech Marketing Challenges

| Challenge | Implication | Strategy |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Regulatory compliance** | Every claim needs legal review | Build compliance into QA process |
| **Trust deficit** | People are cautious with money | Over-invest in proof and credibility |
| **Complex products** | Hard to explain simply | Education-first content strategy |
| **Security concerns** | Prospects worry about data | Prominent security certifications |
| **Long decision cycles** | B2B fintech sales take months | Nurture sequences + thought leadership |

### Fintech-Specific Content Strategy

| Content Type | Purpose | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **Regulatory guides** | Authority + SEO | "Complete guide to [regulation] compliance" |
| **ROI calculators** | Lead gen + proof of value | "Calculate your savings with [Product]" |
| **Security / compliance pages** | Trust building | SOC 2, ISO 27001 certifications |
| **Customer case studies** | Social proof with numbers | "How [Bank] reduced fraud by 40%" |
| **Industry reports** | Thought leadership | Annual State of [Fintech Niche] report |
| **Comparison content** | Bottom-funnel | "[Product] vs. legacy [alternative]" |

### Fintech Compliance Checklist for Marketing

| Requirement | Application |
|------------|-----------|
| **No guaranteed returns** | Never promise specific financial outcomes |
| **Fair lending disclosures** | Required in consumer lending marketing |
| **Privacy policy prominence** | Clearly accessible on all lead capture forms |
| **Terms and conditions** | Link required on all promotional offers |
| **FDIC/SIPC disclosures** | Required if handling deposits/securities |
| **APR disclosures** | Required in credit/lending product advertising |
| **Risk disclaimers** | Required for investment products |

## Part 5: Professional Services / Consulting

### Professional Services Marketing Levers

| Lever | Why It Works | Execution |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| **Thought leadership** | Trust = contracts | Weekly LinkedIn + monthly long-form |
| **Speaking + events** | Stage = authority | Conference circuit + owned webinars |
| **Case studies** | Results = proof | Detailed outcome stories with metrics |
| **Referral network** | Warm intros close faster | Formal referral program + partner network |
| **Methodology branding** | Proprietary process = differentiation | Name your framework, publish it |

### Services Firm Content Priorities

| Content Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|-------------|:---------:|---------|
| **POV articles** (thought leadership) | Weekly | Establish expertise and differentiation |
| **Case studies** | Monthly | Proof of outcomes |
| **Webinars / workshops** | Monthly | Lead generation + authority |
| **Research / benchmarks** | Quarterly | Industry leadership |
| **Guest appearances** (podcasts, publications) | Monthly | Borrowed audience |
| **Email newsletter** | Weekly/Bi-weekly | Nurture existing network |

### Services Firm Sales Funnel

| Stage | Content/Touchpoint | Conversion Target |
|-------|-------------------|:-----------------:|
| **Awareness** | Thought leadership, speaking, referrals | — |
| **Interest** | Newsletter signup, webinar attendance | 5-10% |
| **Consideration** | Case study download, discovery call | 15-25% |
| **Proposal** | Custom proposal + presentation | 30-50% |
| **Close** | Contract negotiation | 40-60% |
