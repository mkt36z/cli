## Part 1: Education Strategy

### Why Build a Marketing Education Platform

| Business Benefit | How Education Delivers It |
|-----------------|-------------------------|
| **Lead generation** | Courses attract ICP who self-identify by enrolling |
| **Product adoption** | Education teaches users how to get value from the product |
| **Brand authority** | Becoming the learning destination builds trust at scale |
| **Community** | Learners form community bonds with the brand |
| **Retention** | Educated users get more value, stay longer |
| **Differentiation** | Comprehensive education is hard to replicate |

### Education Platform Tiers

| Tier | Content | Access | Purpose |
|------|---------|:------:|---------|
| **Free courses** | Introductory, foundational | Open to all | Lead gen, brand awareness |
| **Product certification** | Product-specific, hands-on | Customers | Adoption, retention |
| **Premium courses** | Advanced, expert-level | Paid or premium tier | Revenue, authority |
| **Partner / agency certification** | Partner ecosystem training | Partners | Ecosystem development |

## Part 2: Course Design Framework

### The Learning Path Architecture

```
Learning Paths:
  ├── Marketing Foundations (Beginner)
  │     ├── Course 1: Positioning & Messaging
  │     ├── Course 2: Content Marketing Basics
  │     ├── Course 3: Social Media for Business
  │     └── Assessment → Foundation Certificate
  │
  ├── Growth Marketing (Intermediate)
  │     ├── Course 4: Demand Generation
  │     ├── Course 5: Email Marketing Mastery
  │     ├── Course 6: Paid Acquisition
  │     └── Assessment → Growth Certificate
  │
  ├── Marketing Leadership (Advanced)
  │     ├── Course 7: Marketing Strategy
  │     ├── Course 8: Analytics & Attribution
  │     ├── Course 9: Team Building & Ops
  │     └── Assessment → Leadership Certificate
  │
  └── Specialist Tracks
        ├── SEO Specialist
        ├── Brand Strategy
        ├── Product Marketing
        └── Agency Operations
```

### Course Structure Template

| Module | Duration | Format | Content |
|:------:|:--------:|--------|---------|
| 1 | 15-30 min | Video + reading | Introduction + core concept |
| 2 | 15-30 min | Video + exercise | Framework deep dive |
| 3 | 15-30 min | Video + template | Application to real scenarios |
| 4 | 15-30 min | Video + exercise | Advanced techniques |
| 5 | 30-45 min | Hands-on project | Apply learning to your own business |
| — | 15 min | Quiz / assessment | Knowledge validation |

### Course Design Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Outcome-first** | Every course starts with "By the end, you'll be able to [specific skill]" |
| **Learn by doing** | 50%+ of time spent on exercises and projects, not passive video |
| **Real examples** | Use real case studies, real campaigns, real data |
| **Progressive complexity** | Start simple, layer on complexity |
| **Immediate applicability** | Students should apply learning to their own work within the course |
| **Social learning** | Peer discussion, community forums, cohort elements |
| **Spaced repetition** | Key concepts reinforced across multiple modules |

## Part 3: Certification Program

### Certification Design

| Element | Specification |
|---------|-------------|
| **Certification name** | Marketing36z [Track] Certified |
| **Prerequisites** | Completion of required courses in the learning path |
| **Assessment** | Multiple-choice quiz (80% pass) + practical project |
| **Validity** | 12-24 months (requires renewal) |
| **Digital badge** | Credly or similar — shareable on LinkedIn |
| **Directory** | Public directory of certified professionals |

### Certification Levels

| Level | Requirements | Badge | Benefits |
|-------|-------------|:-----:|---------|
| **Foundation** | Complete Foundations path + assessment | 🟢 | Digital badge, community access |
| **Professional** | Complete 2+ paths + project portfolio | 🔵 | Badge, early access to features, network |
| **Expert** | Complete 3+ paths + case study published | 🟣 | Badge, speaking opportunities, advisory |
| **Instructor** | Expert level + teaching assessment | ⭐ | Badge, revenue share on content, platform access |

## Part 4: Content Production for Education

### Course Content Types

| Type | Format | Production Effort | Best For |
|------|--------|:-:|---------|
| **Video lessons** | 5-15 min video (talking head + slides) | Medium | Concept teaching, demonstrations |
| **Reading material** | Markdown / PDF | Low | Frameworks, reference material |
| **Templates** | Fillable worksheets | Low | Practical application |
| **Exercises** | Guided practice activities | Medium | Skill building |
| **Projects** | Multi-step real-world application | Low (design), High (grading) | Deep learning |
| **Quizzes** | Multiple choice / short answer | Low | Knowledge verification |
| **Case studies** | Written analysis + discussion | Medium | Strategic thinking |
| **Community discussions** | Forum prompts | Very Low | Peer learning |

### Production Schedule

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|:--------:|-----------|
| **Planning** | 2 weeks | Define learning outcomes, outline modules, identify SMEs |
| **Content creation** | 4-6 weeks | Write scripts, record videos, design exercises |
| **Review** | 1-2 weeks | SME review, test with beta learners |
| **Production** | 1-2 weeks | Edit videos, format materials, build in platform |
| **Launch** | 1 week | Announce, enroll early access, monitor feedback |
| **Iteration** | Ongoing | Update based on feedback, refresh content annually |

## Part 6: Monetization Models

| Model | How It Works | Best For |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Free (lead gen)** | All courses free, capture email on enrollment | Brand building, top-of-funnel |
| **Freemium** | Foundation courses free, advanced courses paid | Upsell to power users |
| **Certification fee** | Courses free, assessment/certification costs $ | Revenue from serious learners |
| **Subscription** | Monthly/annual access to all courses | Ongoing education platform |
| **Cohort-based premium** | Live cohort courses at premium pricing | High-value expert education |
| **B2B / team licenses** | Company buys access for their team | Enterprise and agency |

## Part 7: Education Platform Checklist

### Phase 1: Foundation
- [ ] Define 3-5 learning paths aligned with product value
- [ ] Create first course (most popular / highest-demand topic)
- [ ] Choose platform (Teachable, Thinkific, custom)
- [ ] Build assessment and certification process
- [ ] Launch with beta cohort (10-20 learners)

### Phase 2: Growth
- [ ] Build out remaining learning paths
- [ ] Launch certification program with digital badges
- [ ] Create community space for learners
- [ ] Develop partner/agency certification track
- [ ] Integrate education completion with product (feature unlocks, badges)

### Phase 3: Scale
- [ ] Instructor program (community-created content)
- [ ] Multi-language courses
- [ ] Enterprise/team licensing
- [ ] Continuing education / renewal program
- [ ] Analytics dashboard for learning outcomes + business impact
